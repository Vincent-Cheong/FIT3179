Worldwide Covid-19 Cases

How does the world look now?

The choropleth map below gives a good overall view of the virus outbreak.





America is the top for number of confirmed cases. Many other countries suffer more than thousands of cases. Few countries have very few number of cases. For example, Greenland has the least cases. Perhaps, the cold weather there has connection to it.


Each on its own.

The bubble plot below provides a good interface to see the countries' deaths and recoveries by region and/or population.





African countries appear to be clustered more closely together in terms of recovery-death ratio. The Americas are more diverse in the ratio where some are extremely low while some are extremly high. The rest of the continents have relatively close number of deaths and recoveries.
